# Vynixius

**Vynixius** is a powerful Roblox script hub, previously known as **Astral**. We have since rebranded to establish a stronger identity and to better represent the quality and direction of our project.

Our philosophy is simple: **“Quality over Quantity.”**
We don’t bloat our script with unnecessary or useless features. Instead, we focus on well-designed, practical utility tools that genuinely improve the experience.

Vynixius is **free to use**, with optional paid enhancements available for users who want additional functionality.

## Compatibility

Vynixius supports most major Roblox executors on the market and is built with broad compatibility in mind.

## Supported Games

The list of games supported by Vynixius include:

* Jailbreak
* Doors
* Murder Mystery 2
* Build A Boat For Treasure
* Piggy
* Tower of Hell
* Forsaken
* 99 Nights in the Forest

..and more!
